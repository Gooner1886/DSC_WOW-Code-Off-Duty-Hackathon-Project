Chamundeshwari Temple

Built atop the Chamundi Hills, this temple dates back to the 12th century. The idol of the main deity is made of gold.


26km from city centre

Somanathapura Temple

The village is named after Somnath, the commander of the Hoysala army who founded this place.It has a perfect natural setting for a family picnic.


"Kashi of the South"

Talakadu

Nestled on the banks of River Cauvery, Talakadu is quite famous among the devotees who come here for a special worship.


2km from city centre

Trinesvaraswamy Temple

This ancient temple is located outside the Mysore Fort, the main deity being Trinesvara i.e. the three eyed Shiva.


41km from city centre

Melukote Temples

The two temples in Melukote are Tirunarayana temple and Yoga Narasimha, both located at the foothills and top respectively of the Yadavgiri hills.


20km from city centre

Srikantesvara Temple

This temple built in the Dravidian style is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is said to have healing powers. The 125 feet tall Gopura along with the 7 gold kalash are a must see on visiting this temple.
